    LOCATION

    ABOUT THIS PLACE

    The House Of The Way (THOTW) Inn & Convention Center is located near the Brgy. Hall of Brgy.XI, Mt. Carmel Church, Filamer Christian University, Capiz Evangelical Church, City Mall, & Robinson's Mall. It is approximately 3-5 miles from the airport and the beach.

    The Space

    The room is located at the 3rd floor of THOTW, approximately 3 flights, good for 1 guest and is available for weekly or monthly use.

    Guest Access

    The place is at the third floor with approximately 3 flights, no elevator or escalator. The small kitchen is available at the 3rd floor and the washing machine is available to be used for P150.00 per load using your own detergent. Please ask to use the washing machine when using for the first time.

    Other things to note

    THOTW is approximately 5 km. from the beach. The city is usually quiet after 9 PM, the malls close at 8 PM and the tricycle is usually the available transportation to go around the city. There are several restaurants nearby that are open until 10 PM near Robinson's mall: Bread Basket, Sigpum, & Cafe Terraza, approximately 1 km./5 minutes from THOTW. There are also restaurants inside Robinsons mall. The restaurants at the beach are open at night but transportation may be a challenge unless a private car is available. SM mall is approximately 4-5 km./15-16 min. by car from THOTW.
