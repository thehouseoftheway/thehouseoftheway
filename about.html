    ABOUT US

    Welcome to The House of the Way, a sanctuary inspired by the profound wisdom of John 14:6, where Jesus declared, "I am the way, the truth, and the life." This establishment embodies the principles of guidance, truth, and community, serving as a haven for travelers and a gathering place for those seeking connection and purpose. At The House of the Way, we strive to create an environment that reflects these values, offering exceptional hospitality and a welcoming atmosphere.

    Mission & Vision

    Our Mission

    "Our mission is to provide not only a place to stay but also a space for inspiration, growth, and fellowship, inviting guests to experience the transformative power of faith and community. Through our mission, we aim to be a place where every guest can find rest, renewal, and inspiration on their journey."

    Our Vision

    "The House of the Way Inn and Convention Center envisions becoming a premier destination that fosters connection, inspiration, and growth for all who visit. Rooted in the values of community and faith, our vision is to provide a warm and inviting space where guests feel at home, regardless of their background or purpose for visiting. Through this vision, The House of the Way aims to be a beacon of hope and a nurturing space where everyone can discover their path and purpose."
